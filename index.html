Biography

Li Weigang is a professor and director of Transportation Intelligent Computing Laboratory (TransLab) of the Department of Computer Science (CIC) at the University of Brasilia (UnB), Brazil. In 1994, he received Ph.D. degree from the Aeronautics Institute of Technology (ITA), Brazil. Currently, he is a senior member of IEEE and PQ researcher from Brazilian National Council for Scientific and Technological Development (CNPq). He has coordinated various R&D projects supported by some promotion foundations and also the industry partners, such as Atech, Boeing, CAPES, CNPq and FINEP. He advised more than 110 students including PhD students and Postdoctoral researchers.


Research Fields and Publications

His main research interest is Artificial Intelligence in Air Traffic Control/Management (ATC/ATM), Intelligent Transportation System (ITS) in Air and Urban transportation, Machine Learning in Natural Language Processing (NLP), online social network, and electronica invoice applications.

  • From 2013 to 2015, TransLab coordinated a project of FINEP to develop Airport Departure Management System (SIGePA), see Figure 11, together with Atech Foundation (industry developer), CISCEA of Brazilian Air Force (government user).

  • From 1998 to 2013, prof. Li Weigang was awarded CNPq fellowship (PQ 2). He also served as the coordinator of graduate program (2003-02, 2008-09) and chair (2018-2022) of the Department of Computer Science of UnB.
