Biography

Li Weigang is a professor and director of Transportation Intelligent Computing Laboratory (TransLab) of the Department of Computer Science (CIC) at the University of Brasilia (UnB), Brazil. In 1994, he received Ph.D. degree from the Aeronautics Institute of Technology (ITA), Brazil. Currently, he is a senior member of IEEE and PQ researcher from Brazilian National Council for Scientific and Technological Development (CNPq). He has coordinated various R&D projects supported by some promotion foundations and also the industry partners, such as Atech, Boeing, CAPES, CNPq and FINEP. He advised more than 110 students including undergraduate, master, PhD students and Postdoctoral researchers. His research direction related to artificial intelligence and machine learning in intelligent transportation, air traffic control, multimodal information processing and others.


Research Fields and Publications

His main research interest is Artificial Intelligence in Air Traffic Control/Management (ATC/ATM), Intelligent Transportation System (ITS) in Air and Urban transportation, Machine Learning in Natural Language Processing (NLP), online social network, and electronica invoice applications.

Following links show the selected publications: ITS in ATC/ATM; Reinforcement learning in ATC/ATM; ITS in urban transportation; Machine learning; Online Social Networks NLP & Electronic Invoice.
